Perinatal Pal

This site contains resources for perinatal care providers to help in the understanding of important perinatal concepts as a means to promote, protect, and support normal birth. Current topics covered are cardinal movements of labor, fetal positions, stations of the presenting part, and how to perform Leopold's maneuvers.
